## Recurrence Equations
A ''difference equation'', or ''recurrence equation'', 

Examples:
- Fibonacci Numbers:
- $F_n = F_{n - 1} + F_{n-2}$


[Eigendecomposition](Eigendecomposition) is a good way of solving these equations
